Why an E-Scooter Is Perfect for Students

College costs enough without adding daily bus fares or fuel bills. An e-scooter costs just cents to charge and nothing to park, making it by far the most affordable way to get around.

They fold in seconds and weigh under 15kg, so students can carry them into lectures, the library, or a cramped apartment. No bike lock drama, no theft worries.

This generation cares about the planet. Giving an e-scooter says you support their values while solving a real everyday problem at universities like TCD, UCD, and DCU.

How Students Will Use It

  • Campus commute: Beat the morning rush from digs to lectures in minutes, not half an hour on Dublin Bus.
  • Library runs: Zip from one end of campus to the other between study sessions without breaking a sweat.
  • Part-time job: Get to and from evening shifts without relying on infrequent late-night buses.
  • Socialising: Meet friends across town without calling a taxi or waiting for the Nitelink.
  • Grocery runs: Nip to Aldi or Lidl and back in minutes — no car needed, no bus bags hassle.
  • Last-mile from DART or bus: Unfold at the stop and cover the final stretch home in under five minutes.
