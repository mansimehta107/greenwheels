## How You'll Use It Together

### Adventures for Two, Starting Right Now

- **Weekend Getaway Rides:** Turn a Saturday morning into a proper mini-adventure. Pick a direction, ride for an hour, find a village pub or a coffee shop you've never tried, and take the scenic route home. No booking, no planning — just go.
- **Coastal Exploring in Wicklow & Howth:** The east coast is made for this. Ride out to Howth for fresh seafood, cruise along Bray seafront, or tackle the quieter roads around Greystones. Pack a picnic and make a day of it.
- **Date Night Rides Through Dublin:** Dinner in town doesn't have to end with a taxi queue. Glide home together along the quays, past the lights on the Liffey, and through streets that feel completely different at night.
- **Farmers' Market Trips:** Saturday mornings at Temple Bar Food Market, the Dublin Food Co-op, or the Milk Market in Limerick are infinitely better when you're not circling for parking. Ride in, load up, ride home together.
- **Countryside Adventures Beyond the City:** Ireland's greenways and quiet country roads are perfect for a longer ride. The Royal Canal Way, the Waterford Greenway, or the lanes around Killiney — all within easy reach and all better when shared.
- **Sunset Rides Along the Coast:** There are few things more grounding than a ride along Sandymount Strand or Dún Laoghaire pier as the sun drops. No agenda, no rush — just golden light and good company.
