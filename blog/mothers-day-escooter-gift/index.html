Give Mum the Freedom She Deserves This Mother's Day

Forget the flowers and chocolates — this year, give her something that lasts. A lightweight electric scooter puts the wind in her hair and the world at her feet.

Shop E-Scooters for Mum

Why Mum Will Love an E-Scooter

Mums spend most of their time looking after everyone else. An electric scooter gives her something back — genuine independence. No more waiting for the bus, battling for parking, or relying on someone for a lift.

These scooters are incredibly easy to ride. Step on, push off, and go. Within five minutes she'll feel completely confident. They're lightweight enough to carry up a few steps and fold flat for the boot of the car.

And there's the fresh air factor. A quick spin around the neighbourhood on a spring morning is better than any cup of coffee. It's fun, it's freeing, and it's the kind of self-care that actually fits into a busy life.

How Mum Will Use It

  • Quick trips to the shops — skip the car and the parking hassle, be there and back in minutes
  • Morning fresh air rides — a 15-minute spin before the house wakes up, better than any alarm clock
  • Visiting friends and family — pop over for tea without booking a taxi or hunting for car keys
  • The school run, simplified — turn the drop-off into a quick, traffic-free trip instead of a stressful crawl
  • Exploring the neighbourhood — that new coffee shop, the farmers' market, the park she's been meaning to visit
  • Weekend leisure rides — Saturday along the Liffey, a Sunday loop through Fairview Park — pure bliss

Easy to Ride, Easy to Love

We've chosen models that prioritise comfort and simplicity. The Aovopro scooters weigh under 13kg and fold in seconds — light enough to carry one-handed and small enough to tuck behind the front door.

Solid tyres mean zero punctures and zero maintenance. Just charge, unfold, and ride. For mums who prefer a seated ride, the Enguie T14 folding e-bike offers a comfortable saddle and 35km of range.

Every model we sell is tested on Irish roads, backed by our Dublin service centre, and covered by a full 1-year warranty. Mum can ride with complete peace of mind from day one.
