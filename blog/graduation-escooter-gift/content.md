## How They'll Use It

### A Graduate's New Best Friend

- **Commuting to their first job:** No more cramming onto a packed Luas at 8am or paying €10 a day for city-centre parking. An e-scooter gets them to the office in minutes, rain or shine, with zero fuel costs.
- **Exploring a new city:** Whether they've landed a role in Dublin, Cork, Galway, or Limerick, a scooter is the fastest way to learn a new city — discovering shortcuts, coffee spots, and neighbourhoods the bus doesn't reach.
- **Postgrad campus life:** Heading straight into a master's or PhD? An e-scooter is the quickest way between lectures, the library, and home — and it folds under a desk when they get there.
- **Social life without the taxi bill:** Meeting friends for dinner, heading to a gig, or catching a late film — they can zip across town and back without watching a taxi meter climb or waiting for a night bus.
- **Saving serious money on transport:** At just cents per charge, an e-scooter can save a young professional hundreds of euros a year compared to public transport passes, car running costs, or ride-hailing apps.
- **Weekend adventures:** From the Royal Canal Greenway to the seafront in Dún Laoghaire, Ireland's trails and cycle paths are perfect for a weekend scoot — fresh air, no traffic, and no emissions.
